# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Resumy to be an open, respectful, and inclusive project for everyone.

All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to interact professionally and respectfully.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication
- Assume good intent and provide helpful feedback
- Focus on ideas, not personal attacks
- Welcome contributors from different backgrounds and experience levels
- Accept feedback gracefully

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal insults, intimidation, or threats
- Sexualized language or unwanted advances
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels related to this repository.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report to maintainers through a GitHub issue with limited details and the prefix "conduct:". Share enough context for review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for reviewing reports and taking fair, appropriate action.

Possible actions include:

- Warning
- Temporary restriction from participation
- Permanent removal from community spaces for severe or repeated violations
